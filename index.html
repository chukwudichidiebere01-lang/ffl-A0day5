-ER Verbs: Habiter, Aimer, Partager
Activity 3 of 7 · The most important verb pattern in French. Learn it once, use it forever.

The -ER Pattern Rule

Take any -ER verb. Remove -ER. Add the right ending for each pronoun. That is it. Thousands of French verbs work this way.

Endings: -e · -es · -e · -ons · -ez · -ent
💡 je/il/elle and ils/elles endings are SILENT. Only the spelling is different.
